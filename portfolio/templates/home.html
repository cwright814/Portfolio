My Projects
Trash House YouTube

Trash House is a professional 3D simulation designed in Unity for Wilmington University. It stems from a field trip their Sociology students would take to a purposefully trashed house. The goal - search for evidence that partains to a case study i.e. potential child abuse. Trash House recreates the experience for online and disabled students - fully controlled by mouse or touch screen and maintained by a custom web & database backend for instructors.
